# Hub as dual-identity statement

The root page (`/`) is not a redirect to `/pro` and not a neutral router. It is a deliberate identity statement: the one place on the site where both sides of Michael Schempp — the software architect and the pinball restorer — are visible together as facets of one person. Defaulting visitors straight to the professional section would imply the hobby is secondary or hidden; the Hub makes the dual identity explicit and intentional.
