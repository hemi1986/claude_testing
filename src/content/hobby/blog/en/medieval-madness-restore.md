Medieval Madness by Williams (1997) is widely regarded as one of the greatest pinball machines ever made. My example arrived in a... let's call it "authentically aged" condition.

## Initial state

- Main board: several cold solder joints, one failed transistor
- Playfield: dirty but structurally intact
- Ramps: one broken, one cracked
- Sound: working but scratchy

## Diagnosis

Using the WPC diagnostic tool (serial connection to the Aux board) I was able to systematically test all switches and coils. Saved a lot of time.
